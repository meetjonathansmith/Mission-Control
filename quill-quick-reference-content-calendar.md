## BATCH CREATION WORKFLOW

### Monday Morning (2 hours): Script Writing
**Template for each video (150-200 words):**

```
HOOK (5-7 words):
[Write scroll-stopping opening]

PROBLEM SETUP (2-3 sentences):
[Describe the pain point your audience feels]

YOUR SOLUTION (3-4 sentences):
[Introduce your framework or perspective]

EXPLANATION (4-5 sentences):
[Teach the concept or tell the story]

CALL TO ACTION (1-2 sentences):
[What you want them to do/think]
```

**Pro Tip:** Voice note ideas â†’ Paste into ChatGPT â†’ Ask for script structure â†’ Refine

### Monday Afternoon (90 minutes): Recording
- Set up phone on tripod
- Good lighting (natural or ring light)
- Scripts on laptop (off-camera as prompts)
- Record all 5 videos back-to-back
- Multiple takes OK (edit later)

### Tuesday (2-3 hours): Editing
- Import to editing app (CapCut/Premiere Rush)
- Add text overlays (progressive reveals)
- Trim dead space
- Add transitions
- Export for each platform (9:16 vertical)

### Wednesday-Friday: Post + Engage
- Post 1x daily (optimal times: 7-9am, 12-2pm, 7-10pm ET)
- First hour: respond to ALL comments
- Throughout day: monitor DMs
- End of day: track performance metrics

## MONETIZATION ROADMAP

### Phase 1: Foundation (0-10K followers)
**Timeline:** Months 1-6  
**Focus:** Build credibility, test content  
**Monetization:** 
- Inbound only (don't chase sales)
- Simple offer: 1-on-1 coaching at $2-3K/month
- Goal: 1-2 clients = $2-6K/month

**Content Mix:**
- 60% Education (Pillar 2)
- 30% Proof (Pillar 1)
- 10% BTS (Pillar 4)

### Phase 2: Growth (10-50K followers)
**Timeline:** Months 6-18  
**Focus:** Scale reach, launch first product  
**Monetization:**
- Group program ($1-2K)
- Digital product ($50-200)
- 1-on-1 selective ($3-5K/month)
- Goal: $10-20K/month

**Content Mix:**
- 40% Education (Pillar 2)
- 25% Proof (Pillar 1)
- 15% Leadership (Pillar 3)
- 10% BTS (Pillar 4)
- 10% Monetization (Pillar 5)

### Phase 3: Scale (50-100K followers)
**Timeline:** Months 18-36  
**Focus:** Business systems, team building  
**Monetization:**
- Multiple digital products ($5-10K/month)
- B2B retainers ($10-20K/month)
- Speaking gigs ($5K+ each)
- Selective 1-on-1 ($5K+/month)
- Goal: $30-50K/month

**Content Mix:**
- 35% Education (Pillar 2)
- 20% Proof (Pillar 1)
- 20% Leadership (Pillar 3)
- 10% BTS (Pillar 4)
- 15% Monetization (Pillar 5)

### Phase 4: Authority (100K+ followers)
**Timeline:** Months 36+  
**Focus:** Leverage, licensing, premium positioning  
**Monetization:**
- Premium speaking ($10-25K+)
- High-ticket programs ($5-10K)
- Licensing/franchising
- Book deals
- Strategic partnerships
- Goal: $80K+/month

**Content Mix:**
- 30% Education (Pillar 2)
- 15% Proof (Pillar 1)
- 30% Leadership (Pillar 3)
- 10% BTS (Pillar 4)
- 15% Monetization (Pillar 5)
