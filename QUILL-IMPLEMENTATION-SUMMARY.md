## ðŸš¨ COMMON PITFALLS TO AVOID

1. **Posting Inconsistently**
   - Death for algorithm
   - Solution: Batch record, schedule posts

2. **Being Too Salesy**
   - Turns people off
   - Solution: Educate first, sell never (let them DM you)

3. **Copying Other People's Results**
   - Not authentic
   - Solution: Show YOUR journey, YOUR numbers

4. **Ignoring Comments**
   - Kills engagement
   - Solution: Reply within first hour of posting

5. **Perfect-Waiting Syndrome**
   - Never post because it's not perfect
   - Solution: Post at 80% ready, iterate based on feedback

6. **No Clear CTA**
   - People watch but don't engage
   - Solution: Every post ends with question or prompt

7. **All Work, No Personality**
   - Boring, forgettable
   - Solution: Mix lifestyle posts, show who you are
