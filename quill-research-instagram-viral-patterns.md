## DATA TRACKING & OPTIMIZATION FRAMEWORK

### Key Metrics to Track (Per Caleb Ralston's Method)

**Establish Baseline Performance:**
- Average views per post (by platform)
- Average engagement rate
- Average saves per 1K views
- Average profile visits per post

**Report on Multipliers:**
- 2x+ = Viral outlier (study and replicate)
- 1.5x = Strong performer (make more like this)
- 1x-1.4x = Baseline (maintain quality)
- 0.5x-0.9x = Underperformer (iterate or cut)
- <0.5x = Cut this format/topic

**Leading Indicators:**
- 3-second hold rate (60%+ is strong)
- Watch time percentage
- Saves (highest intent signal)
- Shares (endorsement signal)
- Profile visits (consideration)

**Lagging Indicators:**
- Followers gained
- Website clicks
- DM volume
- Lead form submissions

### A/B Testing Priorities

**Test Monthly (in this order):**
1. Hooks (same content, different opening)
2. Thumbnails (visual pattern interrupts)
3. CTAs (save vs. share vs. DM)
4. Post times (audience-specific)
5. Caption length (short vs. story)

## KEY TAKEAWAYS FOR REAL ESTATE INVESTOR CONTENT

### 1. **Volume > Perfection (Initially)**
- Your first 90 days are data collection
- Audience tells you what's quality, not your gut
- Post 5-7x/week minimum to get statistically significant data
- Use accordion method to contract once you have insights

### 2. **Specificity = Authority**
- Generic: "Great investment opportunity"
- Authoritative: "$89K duplex, $31K rehab, $3,400/mo gross, 14.2% CoC"
- Numbers, timelines, and mistakes build more trust than hype

### 3. **The 3-Second Rule is Law**
- 60%+ of viewers must watch first 3 seconds
- Hook must create curiosity gap or promise immediate value
- Visual + verbal hook must work together
- Test 3-5 hook variations per concept

### 4. **Documentation > Declaration**
- Show property walkthroughs, not just thumbnails
- Film deal analysis in real-time
- Share contractor conversations
- Document failures, not just wins
- Vlogs are depth plays that build core audience trust

### 5. **Platform Context Matters**
- Instagram: Visual-first, polished grid, raw stories
- TikTok/Reels: Fast pace, trending audio, text overlays
- YouTube: Long-form authority, SEO titles
- LinkedIn: Professional storytelling, written depth
- Don't post same format everywhere

### 6. **The 80/20 Content Split**
- 80%: Core real estate investing expertise
- 20%: Personal interests/life (hobbies, family, behind-scenes)
- Makes you interesting human, not just content robot
- Provides multiple "at bats" for audience connection
- Prevents creator burnout

### 7. **Education + Vulnerability = Trust**
- Share 80% of knowledge freely
- Reserve 20% for implementation/templates (paid)
- Show mistakes and lessons
- Challenge guru myths with your real experience
- Position offers as accelerators, not secrets

### 8. **CTAs Should Feel Like Opportunities**
- "DM me for the calculator" > "Buy my course"
- "Save this for when you're ready" > "Sign up now"
- "Tag someone who needs this" > Generic ask
- Lead with value, soft-sell at end
- Make CTA contextual to content topic

### 9. **Repetition Through Story Variance**
- Same core concepts, different scenarios
- Use client/property stories to illustrate principles
- Audience needs to hear things 7+ times to internalize
- Don't fear "saying it again"â€”fear saying it boringly

### 10. **Hire for Bottlenecks, Not Org Charts**
- First hire: What's stopping you from posting more?
- If it's editing time â†’ hire editor
- If it's idea generation â†’ hire content strategist
- If it's filming â†’ hire videographer
- Specialists > generalists once you have budget
