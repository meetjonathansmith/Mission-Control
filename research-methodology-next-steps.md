## üîç WHAT TO LOOK FOR IN EACH VIDEO

### **Hook Analysis (0-10 seconds):**
- **Exact words spoken** - write them down verbatim
- **Visual elements** - what's on screen?
- **Hook type** - Question? Bold claim? Shock stat? Story?
- **Emotional trigger** - Curiosity? Anger? Hope? Fear?
- **Promise made** - What does the viewer expect to get?

### **Structure Analysis:**
- **Time stamps for major sections** - when do they shift topics?
- **Pacing** - Fast cuts? Long takes? Mix?
- **Visual aids** - Whiteboard? Screen share? B-roll?
- **Repetition** - Do they summarize? Callback to earlier points?

### **Retention Analysis:**
- **Open loops** - "I'll tell you in 3 minutes..." (note the timestamp)
- **Pattern interrupts** - When do they change visual style?
- **Reveals** - When do they drop new information?
- **Progress markers** - "We're halfway through..."

### **Engagement Analysis:**
- **CTA placement** - When do they ask for engagement?
- **CTA specificity** - "Comment X" vs. "Leave a comment"
- **Top comments** - What are people quoting back?
- **Reply rate** - Does the creator engage in comments?

### **Production Analysis:**
- **Setting** - Home office? Studio? Outdoor?
- **Lighting** - Natural? Studio? Mix?
- **Audio** - Mic quality (this matters more than video)
- **Editing style** - Jump cuts? Smooth? Graphics heavy?

## üí° HYPOTHESES TO TEST (Based on Current Frameworks)

### **Hypothesis 1: Hook Type Impact**
- **Test:** Create 3 videos on same topic with different hook types
  - Video A: Bold claim hook
  - Video B: Question hook
  - Video C: Story hook
- **Measure:** Which gets highest CTR?
- **Expected:** Bold claim or question will outperform story (based on patterns)

### **Hypothesis 2: Video Length Sweet Spot**
- **Test:** Same framework, different lengths
  - Video A: 8-10 minutes
  - Video B: 12-14 minutes
  - Video C: 15-17 minutes
- **Measure:** Average view duration %
- **Expected:** 12-14 min will have best retention (long enough for depth, short enough to hold attention)

### **Hypothesis 3: CTA Placement**
- **Test:** Place CTA at different points
  - Video A: CTA only at end
  - Video B: CTA mid-video + end
  - Video C: CTA beginning + mid + end
- **Measure:** Comment count and engagement rate
- **Expected:** Multiple CTAs will increase engagement

### **Hypothesis 4: Thumbnail Text**
- **Test:** Same video, different thumbnails
  - Thumbnail A: No text, just image
  - Thumbnail B: 2-3 word text overlay
  - Thumbnail C: Number + benefit text
- **Measure:** CTR
- **Expected:** Number + benefit will have highest CTR

### **Hypothesis 5: Framework Effectiveness by Pillar**
- **Test:** Apply different frameworks to each content pillar
  - Faith content: Test Framework #1 vs. #9
  - Real estate: Test Framework #3 vs. #19
  - Neuroscience: Test Framework #2 vs. #16
- **Measure:** View duration, engagement, shares
- **Expected:** Certain frameworks will significantly outperform for specific topics
