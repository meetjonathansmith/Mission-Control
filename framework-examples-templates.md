## EXAMPLE 4: Neuroscience of Discipline (Framework #2 + #16)

### **Video Title:** "How to Rewire Your Brain in 72 Hours (Dopamine Detox)"

### **Thumbnail:** Brain scan image, "72 HOURS" text, before/after brain comparison

### **FULL OUTLINE:**

**HOOK (0-10 seconds):**
"Your brain physically changes in 72 hours when you cut out these three things. Here's the neuroscience behind it."

**THE SCIENCE (1-3 minutes):**
- "Your brain runs on dopamine - the reward chemical"
- "Modern life hijacks your dopamine system: porn, social media, junk food, video games"
- "These create supernormal stimuli - way more dopamine than natural rewards"
- "Over time, your brain desensitizes. Normal life feels boring."
- **Show brain diagram:** Dopamine pathways

**THE PROBLEM (3-5 minutes):**
- "You need more and more stimulation to feel normal"
- "Your baseline drops. You can't focus. Can't find joy in simple things."
- "You're addicted - not to the substance, but to the dopamine hit"
- "The good news: This is reversible. Fast."

**THE 72-HOUR PROTOCOL (5-9 minutes):**
**Cut these three things for 72 hours:**
1. **All screens (except work):** No phone, no TV, no YouTube
2. **All processed food:** Only whole foods, no sugar
3. **All sexual stimulation:** No porn, no masturbation

**Replace with:**
- Reading (physical books)
- Walking outside
- Prayer/meditation
- Conversation
- Physical work

**THE TIMELINE (9-12 minutes):**
- **Hour 0-6:** Cravings start. Your brain wants the hit.
- **Hour 6-24:** The hardest period. You'll feel anxious, bored.
- **Hour 24-48:** Cravings peak. This is the war.
- **Hour 48-72:** Breakthrough. Clarity starts returning.
- **Post-72:** Your baseline starts resetting. Normal stimuli feel good again.

**THE NEUROSCIENCE (12-13 minutes):**
- "Your dopamine receptors upregulate when you stop overstimulating them"
- "Think of it like rest days for your muscles - recovery creates growth"
- "72 hours is enough to start the reset"
- "21 days to solidify it. 90 days to fully rewire."

**REAL APPLICATION (13-14 minutes):**
- "Pick a weekend. Friday night through Monday morning."
- "Tell people you're offline."
- "Prepare: Have books ready, plan outdoor activities."
- "Track how you feel every 12 hours - journal it."

**CTA:**
"Try this for 72 hours and comment back with what changed. I'm telling you, this works. Your brain will thank you."

**RETENTION TACTICS:**
- Open loop at 2:30: "The third thing you need to cut is what most guys can't do..."
- Visual demonstrations: Brain scans, dopamine pathway animations
- Pattern interrupt at 8:00: Cut to B-roll of outdoor activities (what to do instead)
- Science proof: Cite actual studies on screen
