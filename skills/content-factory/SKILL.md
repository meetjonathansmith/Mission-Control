## SECTION 4: CONTENT RULES (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

1. **Nothing posts without Jonathan's approval.** Alfred and agents create. Jonathan decides.
2. **Document > Teach.** Default to "here's what I did" not "here's what you should do."
3. **Exception: Faith content.** Jonathan has earned authority here. Go direct.
4. **One topic per piece.** Don't try to say everything. Say one thing well.
5. **Hook must create tension in first 2 seconds.** If it doesn't stop the scroll, rewrite.
6. **Every piece must connect back to a core framework.** Even if subtly.
7. **Raw > Polished.** Authenticity beats production value every time.
8. **No engagement bait.** No "comment if you agree." No "tag someone who needs this."
9. **Mistakes and failures get more airtime than wins.** That's the brand differentiator.
10. **Real numbers when sharing deals.** Don't hide the math. Transparency builds trust.
