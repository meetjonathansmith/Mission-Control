## ðŸš¨ CONTENT RULES (DO NOT BREAK)

### Always:
1. **Post consistently** - Same days, same times = algorithm loves you
2. **Front-load value** - First 3 seconds must hook
3. **Use text overlays** - Many scroll with sound off
4. **Direct eye contact** - Build connection through camera
5. **End with CTA** - Always ask question or spark engagement

### Never:
1. **Don't post just to post** - Quality > quantity
2. **Don't be salesy** - Educate first, sell later
3. **Don't copy others' results** - Show YOUR journey
4. **Don't ignore comments** - Engagement = algorithm boost
5. **Don't post without purpose** - Every post has a goal
