## Strategic Recommendations

### Priority 1: Solve the Volume-Response Tradeoff

**The data is clear:**
- Low volume (Jan: 53k texts) = high response (1.52%)
- High volume (Dec/Feb: 110k texts) = low response (~1%)

**Options:**

**A) Accept lower efficiency at scale**
- Keep running 110k texts/month at 0.93% response
- You get ~1,023 conversations vs Jan's 812
- Costs 2x as much per conversation

**B) Optimize for efficiency**
- Cut volume back to 60k texts/month
- Focus on high-performing segments (VA Beach model)
- Target 1.5%+ response rate
- Get similar conversation volume at half the cost

**C) Hybrid approach**
- Segment campaigns: High-performers at max volume, low-performers paused
- Rotate lists to reduce fatigue
- Test message variations to refresh engagement

**My recommendation:** Option C (hybrid). Your current approach is burning budget on diminishing returns.
