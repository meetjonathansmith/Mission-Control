## FILMING TIPS

### Energy Level:
- **Not hyped, not monotone** â†’ Conversational confidence
- Imagine explaining this to a friend who's genuinely curious
- Smile when appropriate (especially Kingdom Business posts)
- Serious tone for vulnerability/lesson posts

### Pacing:
- **Don't rush** â€” You have 60-90 seconds, use it
- Pause between points (makes editing easier)
- Emphasize KEY PHRASES (the text overlays)

### Eye Contact:
- Look directly at camera lens (not at yourself on screen)
- Imagine ONE person on the other side of that lens

### Mistakes:
- If you mess up, PAUSE, then restart that sentence
- Don't restart entire video â€” fix in editing
- Authentic stumbles are okay (shows you're human)

### Multiple Takes:
- Film hook 2-3 times (pick best energy)
- Film full video 1-2 times (pick best delivery)
- If a take feels OFF, redo it â€” you'll know
