## ðŸ“ UNIVERSAL VIDEO STRUCTURE (Fits Any Framework)

### **HOOK (0-10 seconds)**
- Make a bold promise or ask compelling question
- Create curiosity gap
- Signal value immediately

### **SETUP (10-60 seconds)**
- Why this matters
- Who this is for
- What they'll get by watching

### **CONTENT (Main Body - 8-12 minutes)**
- Deliver on the promise
- Use framework structure
- Place open loops every 2-3 minutes
- Pattern interrupt every 30 seconds (visual change, energy shift, example)
- Stack value progressively (each section builds)

### **PROOF (Woven Throughout)**
- Show receipts: screenshots, contracts, photos
- Share testimony: yours or others'
- Cite scripture or science
- Demonstrate, don't just tell

### **CTA (Final 30-60 seconds)**
- Recap key point
- Make specific ask
- Create accountability ("Comment 'DAY 1'")
- Tease next video (if series)

## ðŸŽ¤ DELIVERY TIPS

### **Energy:**
- Stand while filming (increases energy)
- Move your hands (natural, not forced)
- Vary pace (slow for important points, fast for transitions)
- Conviction moments: lean in, get intense

### **Authenticity:**
- Don't "perform" - teach or share
- Be vulnerable when appropriate (transformation stories)
- Show conviction (faith content)
- Be yourself (your weird is your differentiator)

### **Clarity:**
- One idea per sentence
- Pause between major points
- Repeat key concepts
- Use simple language (8th grade reading level)
