## ðŸŽ¯ THE 5 PROVEN HOOK FORMULAS

### 1. BIG NUMBER AUTHORITY (Sam Gaudet)
**Pattern:** [MASSIVE RESULT] + [Teaser of how]

**Templates:**
- "$[X] in [Time Frame]: The [#] [Things] That Made It Happen"
- "[X] [Units/Deals/Results] in [Time]: Here's the Strategy"
- "From $0 to $[X]: The [#]-Phase System"

**Examples:**
- "+3 Billion View Content Strategy"
- "$100M Creative Director: Here's How"
- "100M VIEW YOUTUBE IDEATION"

**When to Use:** Mondays, credibility-building posts, milestone celebrations

---

### 2. DIRECT TACTICAL ADVICE (Briar Cochran)
**Pattern:** [Authority Title]: Stop [Wrong Thing], [Do This Instead]

**Templates:**
- "[Your Title]: Stop [Common Mistake]..."
- "Why [Everyone] Does [X] Wrong (And What to Do)"
- "The [Mistake] Every [Profession] Makes"

**Examples:**
- "Content Strategist: Stop posting..."
- "Content Strategist: Stop sharing your life, start giving value..."
- "Real Estate Investors: Stop posting property photos..."

**When to Use:** Fridays, engagement-driver posts, teaching moments

---

### 3. URGENCY / FOMO (Briar Cochran)
**Pattern:** [Time Constraint] + [Opportunity/Risk]

**Templates:**
- "You Have [X Time] to [Opportunity]"
- "Why [Time Frame] is [Critical/Make-or-Break] for [Audience]"
- "[X] [Markets/Opportunities] I'm Watching RIGHT NOW"

**Examples:**
- "YOU HAVE 2 YEARS LEFT TO BUILD YOUR PERSONAL BRAND"
- "Why this quarter is critical for investors"
- "This window won't last: Here's why"

**When to Use:** Weekends, market commentary, seasonal opportunities

---

### 4. FRAMEWORK / SYSTEM (Sam Gaudet)
**Pattern:** How to [Specific Result] in [Time/Steps]

**Templates:**
- "How to [Outcome] in [X Seconds/Steps]"
- "The [#]-Step [System] I Use for [Result]"
- "My [Framework Name]: [Brief Description]"

**Examples:**
- "How I Analyze a Deal in 60 Seconds"
- "The 5-Phase System to $1M Portfolio"
- "VISUAL BRANDING 101"

**When to Use:** Tuesdays/Thursdays, educational content, building teaching authority

---

### 5. NUMBERED LIST (Both)
**Pattern:** [#] Ways to [Outcome]

**Templates:**
- "[#] Ways to [Result]"
- "[#] [Mistakes/Lessons/Tactics] Every [Audience] Should Know"
- "The [#] [Things] That Changed My [Business/Life]"

**Examples:**
- "4 WAYS TO CREATE CREATE NEW CONTENT"
- "3 Decisions That Built My $2M Portfolio"
- "5 Markets I'm Watching in 2026"

**When to Use:** Any day, highly shareable, easy to consume

## ðŸŽ¨ VISUAL STYLE GUIDE

### SAM GAUDET STYLE (Authority Posts)

**Text Overlays:**
- Font: BOLD, sans-serif (Arial Black, Impact, Montserrat Black)
- Size: HUGE (takes up 40-60% of screen)
- Color: Yellow on Black, Red on White, White on Dark Blue
- Contrast: Maximum, impossible to miss

**Composition:**
- You: Direct-to-camera, centered, eye contact
- Background: Simple, single color or subtle blur
- Lighting: Bright, even, professional
- Energy: High, confident, excited

**Graphics:**
- Pyramids, frameworks, Venn diagrams
- Simple arrows, circles, highlighting
- Phone screen grabs with big numbers
- Graphs with upward trajectories

**When to Use:** Monday authority posts, framework posts, results showcases

---

### BRIAR COCHRAN STYLE (Relatable Posts)

**Text Overlays:**
- Font: Bold but more natural (Helvetica Bold, Open Sans Bold)
- Size: Large but allows for more words
- Color: White or Yellow on darker backgrounds, subtle drop shadow
- Contrast: Good but not aggressive

**Composition:**
- You: More casual, varied angles, conversational
- Background: Real locations (coffee shops, outdoors, offices, properties)
- Lighting: Natural or available light
- Energy: Authentic, varied (serious to lighthearted)

**Graphics:**
- Minimal on-screen text, cleaner look
- Subtitles/captions for clarity
- Podcast aesthetic (mic visible, interview style)
- Real documents, screens, props

**When to Use:** Wednesday lifestyle posts, Friday engagement posts, behind-the-scenes

## ðŸ“Š POST-PERFORMANCE DECODER

**If a post gets:**

**High Views, Low Engagement:**
- Hook worked, content didn't deliver
- **Fix:** Make body as valuable as hook promises

**Low Views, High Engagement:**
- Great content, weak hook
- **Fix:** Make hook bolder, more curiosity-inducing

**High Saves, Low Shares:**
- Valuable but not conversation-worthy
- **Fix:** Add controversial or relatable element

**High Comments, Low Saves:**
- Engaging but not tactical enough
- **Fix:** Add framework or actionable takeaway

**High Shares, Everything Else Low:**
- Relatable/funny but not building authority
- **Fix:** Balance entertainment with education
