### Authenticity Signals (What Makes Content "Real")

**Trust Builders:**
1. **Visible Imperfection**
   - Not overly polished editing
   - Real locations (not studio sets)
   - Authentic emotion/energy
   - Mistakes left in occasionally

2. **Proof Over Claims**
   - Screenshots of results
   - Specific numbers (not "a lot")
   - Client/student testimonials
   - Behind-the-scenes process

3. **Vulnerability**
   - Sharing failures, not just wins

   - Humanizing the journey

4. **Consistency**
   - Regular posting schedule
   - Recognizable format/style
   - Predictable value delivery
   - Long-term presence (not fly-by-night)

   - "This helped me..." vs "This will change your life"

## PLATFORM-SPECIFIC TACTICS

### Instagram Reels Optimization

**Algorithm Priorities (2026):**
1. Hook rate (% who watch past 3 seconds)
2. Retention (average % watched)
3. Replays (viewer watches 2+ times)
4. Saves (strongest signal of value)
5. Shares (especially DM shares)
6. Comments (quality > quantity)

**Technical Specs:**
- 9:16 vertical format
- 30-60 seconds ideal (90 max before reach drops)
- 1080x1920 resolution minimum
- Upload directly (not cross-post from TikTok)
- Trending audio helps discovery (but not required)
- Text overlays on-screen (most watch without sound)

**Posting Strategy:**
- 3-5x/week minimum for growth
- Consistency > frequency
- Best times: 6-9am, 12-2pm, 7-10pm ET
- Avoid late night posts (poor initial performance)

**Caption Strategy:**
- Front-load value (first 125 chars shown without "See more")
- 3-4 relevant hashtags (not 30)
- CTA at end
- Line breaks for readability
- @ mentions at end (not beginning)

---

### TikTok Optimization

**Key Differences from Instagram:**
- Volume rewarded (can post 15x/day if sustainable)
- FYP algorithm more aggressive (easier to go viral from zero)
- Younger demographic (Gen Z vs Millennial)
- Trend-driven (audio/format trends matter more)

**Strategy:**
- Higher posting frequency (7-14x/week)
- Faster pacing (hook in 1 second, not 3)
- On-screen text essential
- Comments/stitches/duets drive reach
- Series content performs well ("Part 1 of...")

---

### LinkedIn (B2B plays)

**Different Rules:**
- **NO** hashtags (LinkedIn deprioritizes them)
- Long-form text posts perform well
- Professional tone, but personality matters
- Commenting > posting for growth
- Thought leadership > entertainment

**Best for:**
- B2B consulting positioning
- Speaking opportunities
- Corporate partnerships
- Professional credibility

## MONETIZATION MODELS (What Works 2026)

### Model 1: Education/Courses (Meagan Hall approach)
- Digital products ($50-500)
- High volume required
- Low-touch delivery
- Scalable once created
- Requires large audience (50K+)

**Revenue Formula:** Price x Daily Sales x 30 days  
Example: $250 product x 10 sales/day = $75K/month

### Model 2: Consulting/Coaching (Jade Sprake/Briar Cochran approach)
- High-ticket 1-on-1 ($2-5K/month per client)
- Low volume required (5-10 clients)
- High-touch delivery
- Not scalable (time-for-money)
- Works with smaller audience (10K+)

**Revenue Formula:** Price x Client Count  
Example: $3K/month x 10 clients = $30K/month

### Model 3: Group Programs (Hybrid)
- Medium-ticket ($1-2K/month)
- Medium volume (20-50 participants)
- Medium-touch delivery
- Somewhat scalable
- Requires moderate audience (20K+)

**Revenue Formula:** Price x Participant Count  
Example: $1K/month x 30 participants = $30K/month

### Model 4: B2B Retainers (Caleb Ralston/Sam Gaudet approach)
- High-ticket ($5-10K/month per company)
- Very low volume (3-8 companies)
- Custom delivery
- Limited scalability
- Requires strong credibility

**Revenue Formula:** Monthly Retainer x Company Count  
Example: $7K/month x 5 companies = $35K/month

### Model 5: Speaking + Licensing (Authority play)
- Premium rates ($10-25K per engagement)
- Occasional income
- Requires 100K+ following + proven results
- 3-5 year build minimum

**Smart Money Stack:**
Combine multiple models for diversified income:
- 3-5 consulting clients ($15K/month)
- 1 digital product ($10K/month passive)
- 2-3 B2B retainers ($15K/month)
- Occasional speaking ($5K/month averaged)
**Total: $45K/month from multiple streams**

## GROWTH TACTICS (PROVEN 2026)

### The Accordion Method (Briar Cochran)
1. Post 1x/day until consistent 1-2K views
2. Once cracked, 2-3x volume (pour gas on fire)
3. When plateau hits, restart with new approach

### 7-Day Content Cycles
- Monday: Educational deep-dive
- Tuesday: Quick tip/hack
- Wednesday: Story/case study
- Thursday: Behind-the-scenes
- Friday: Thought leadership
- Saturday: Personal/fun
- Sunday: Rest or bonus content

### Collaboration Strategy
- Find creators with 50-150% your follower count
- Adjacent niche (not direct competitor)
- Propose win-win collaboration
- Cross-promote to each other's audiences
- Aim for 1-2 collaborations/month

### Email List Building
- Create simple lead magnet (PDF guide, checklist, templates)
- Offer in bio link + video CTAs
- Weekly newsletter minimum
- Aim for 10% follower-to-subscriber ratio
- Email = owned audience (platform-proof)

### Comment Section Strategy
- Pin strategic comments (clarifying questions, song links, community names)
- Plant comments with friends (guide conversation)
- Respond to all comments in first hour
- Ask question in last comment to boost engagement
