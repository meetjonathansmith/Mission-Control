### VIDEO STRUCTURE PATTERNS

**Common Flow Across All 4:**

1. **HOOK (First 15-30 seconds):**
   - Bold statement or question
   - Personal story tease OR shocking stat
   - Promise of what's coming

2. **CREDIBILITY ANCHOR (30 seconds - 2 minutes):**
   - Quick authority establishment (results, credentials, story)
   - Why THIS person on THIS topic
   - NOT a lengthy bio dump

3. **TEACHING CORE (Middle 70%):**
   - **Kyle:** Personal narrative woven with principles
   - **Goshen:** Framework-heavy (3-step paths, hidden foundations)
   - **Myron:** Biblical principle → Business application → Story proof
   - **Rabbi Lapin:** Ancient wisdom → Modern translation → Practical steps

4. **CALL TO ACTION (Final 2-3 minutes):**
   - Recap key points (usually 3)
   - Clear next step (link in description, challenge, community)
   - Spiritual/motivational closing
