## KEY PATTERNS ACROSS ALL CREATORS

### 1. CONTENT PILLARS (Common Themes)
- **Education/Value-First**: 40-50% teaching content
- **Behind-the-Scenes/Process**: 20-30% showing the work
- **Personal Story/Journey**: 15-20% vulnerability and lessons
- **Results/Case Studies**: 10-15% proof and social proof

### 2. HOOK STRUCTURES THAT WIN

**Pattern 1: The Contrarian Hook**
- "Most [people] do [common thing], but the best do [opposite]"
- Example: "Most agents sell everything. The best keep one."

**Pattern 2: The Specific Result**
- "[Number] [result] in [timeframe]"
- Example: "$1.2M in 11 months using micro content"

**Pattern 3: The Question Hook**
- Direct questions that hit pain points
- Example: "How much equity do you own from all those deals you closed?"

**Pattern 4: The Quote-to-Video Strategy (Caleb Ralston)**
- Take your best-performing post quote
- Use it as the hook for a video
- Expand on the story behind it

### 3. FORMAT PATTERNS

**Reels (60% of content)**
- 30-60 seconds optimal (not 90)
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- One clear point per video
- Native captions always
- Face-to-camera builds trust

**Carousels (40% of content)**
- 5-7 slides optimal
- Slide 1: Hook + promise
- Slides 2-6: Value delivery
- Slide 7: Clear CTA
- Use consistent branding

### 4. POSTING CADENCE

**Optimal Schedule:**
- 4-6 posts per week
- 2-3 Reels
- 2-3 Carousels
- Stories daily (behind-scenes)
- Best times: 7-9 AM, 12-2 PM, 5-7 PM EST

### 5. VOICE & TONE PATTERNS

**What Works:**
- Conversational, not corporate
- Confident without arrogance
- Specific over generic
- Stories over statistics
- "I/You" language, not "we/they"

### 6. ENGAGEMENT DRIVERS

**High-Save Content:**
- Step-by-step frameworks
- Checklists and blueprints
- "Save this for later" prompts
- Transformational before/after

**High-Share Content:**
- Mindset shifts
- Contrarian takes
- Relatable struggles
- Success formulas

**High-Comment Content:**
- "Comment [WORD] for..."
- Direct questions
- Controversial (but respectful) takes
- "What would you do?" scenarios

### 7. CTA STRUCTURES THAT CONVERT

**Level 1: Engagement CTAs**
- "Drop a 🔥 if this resonates"
- "Save this if you're ready to..."
- "Share with an agent who needs this"

**Level 2: DM CTAs**
- "Comment [WORD] and I'll send..."
- "DM me [WORD] for the blueprint"
- Simple one-word triggers

**Level 3: Link CTAs**
- Only after value delivery
- "Full training in bio"
- "Free guide at link"

## THE FORMULA THAT WORKS

1. **Open Strong**: Hook in 3 seconds/first line
2. **One Point**: Don't dilute the message
3. **Specific Story**: Real numbers, real people
4. **Clear Lesson**: What they can do today
5. **Simple CTA**: One action, easy to take

Remember: You're documenting the climb, not preaching from the mountain.
